# Getting an OpenRouter API Key

OpenRouter gives you access to AI models for generating stories and illustrations. Here's how to get your API key.

## Step 1: Create an Account

1. Go to [openrouter.ai](https://openrouter.ai)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right
3. You can sign up with Google, GitHub, or email

## Step 2: Add Credits

1. After signing in, click your profile icon → **"Credits"**
2. Click **"Add Credits"**
3. Start with **$5** — this is enough for dozens of stories
4. Enter your payment method and confirm

> **Tip:** $5 in credits typically lasts 1-2 months for a family making a few stories per week.

## Step 3: Get Your API Key

1. Go to [openrouter.ai/keys](https://openrouter.ai/keys)
2. Click **"Create Key"**
3. Give it a name like "Storybook"
4. **Copy the key** — it starts with `sk-or-...`
5. Keep this key private! Don't share it with anyone.

## Step 4: Paste into Storybook

When you first open your Storybook app, the setup wizard will ask for this key. Just paste it in and click "Validate."

The app will test the key to make sure it works before saving it.

## How Much Does It Cost?

OpenRouter charges based on which AI models you use and how much. Rough estimates:

| Activity | Cost |
|----------|------|
| Generate one story (text) | ~$0.01-0.05 |
| Generate illustrations for a story | ~$0.05-0.15 |
| **One complete storybook** | **~$0.10-0.20** |

You can choose cheaper or more expensive models in the app settings. The default models balance quality and cost.

## Troubleshooting

**"Invalid API key"** — Make sure you copied the full key, including the `sk-or-` prefix.

**"Insufficient credits"** — Add more credits at [openrouter.ai/credits](https://openrouter.ai/credits).

**"Rate limited"** — You're generating too fast. Wait a minute and try again.
